What is the Atlantis Project?

We offer internships in public hospitals in Europe. You will have the opportunity to shadow doctors and learn about different medical specialties, see the European healthcare system from the inside, improve language skills, and contribute to the islands by teaching English to locals or volunteering in the community.

Who Can Apply?

Our programs are open to pre-health or pre-med undergraduates, and anyone interested in exploring health related professions.
